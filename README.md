## 💵 Tip Tracker Pro

**Know your real hourly rate — shift by shift.**

# 🚫 NO ADS. EVER.

Tip Tracker Pro is a lightweight, no-nonsense app for anyone who earns tips: bartenders, servers, drivers, stylists, and more. Log your tips and hours after each shift and instantly see what you actually made per hour — not just what landed in the jar.

**✨ Features**

- 📅 **Calendar-first view** — see a full month at a glance, with each day showing tips earned and hourly rate. Tap any day to edit it.
- 📊 **Breakdown by Day / Week / Month / Year / All-Time** — track trends and spot your best-earning shifts.
- ☁️ **Optional cloud sync** — sign in to back up your data and access it from any device. Works fully offline too — no account required.
- ⬇️⬆️ **Export & Import** — your data is yours. Back it up or move it anytime with a simple file export/import.
- 🔒 **Private by default** — runs entirely in your browser with local storage unless you choose to sync.
- ⚡ **Zero install** — no app store, no dependencies, just open and go.

# 🚫 NO ADS. NO TRACKERS. NO CATCH.

**Why Tip Tracker Pro?**
Tip income is unpredictable — the same job can pay wildly different hourly rates depending on the night. Tip Tracker Pro turns your raw shift data into a clear picture of when and where your time is actually worth the most.
